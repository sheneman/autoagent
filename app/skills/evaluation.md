# Evaluation Skill

## Objective
Rigorously assess report quality and provide actionable feedback.

## Scoring Dimensions (1-10 each)

### Accuracy (weight: 25%)
- Are claims supported by cited sources?
- Are statistics correctly presented?
- Is the overall narrative factually sound?

### Completeness (weight: 25%)
- Are all major aspects of the topic covered?
- Are there obvious gaps or missing perspectives?
- Is there sufficient depth on each subtopic?

### Clarity (weight: 20%)
- Is the writing clear and easy to follow?
- Are technical terms explained?
- Is the language appropriate for the audience?

### Structure (weight: 15%)
- Is the report well-organized?
- Do sections flow logically?
- Are headers and formatting used effectively?

### Engagement (weight: 15%)
- Is the report interesting to read?
- Does it highlight surprising or important points?
- Would a reader want to finish it?

## Pass Criteria
- All individual scores >= 7
- Weighted overall score >= 7.5
- No critical factual errors

## Feedback Format
- Start with 2-3 specific strengths
- List specific improvements with referenced sections
- If research gaps exist, provide exact search queries
