# Podcasting Skill

## Objective
Create an engaging two-host podcast script that makes research accessible and fun.

## Format
- Two hosts: Alex (curious, enthusiastic) and Sam (knowledgeable, witty)
- Target length: ~10 minutes (~1500 words of dialogue)
- Every line prefixed with speaker: `ALEX:` or `SAM:`

## Episode Structure
1. **Cold Open** (30 sec): A surprising fact or provocative question
2. **Introduction** (1 min): Hosts introduce the topic and why it matters
3. **Deep Dive Part 1** (3 min): First major finding with discussion
4. **Deep Dive Part 2** (3 min): Second major finding
5. **The "Wait, What?" Moment** (1 min): Most surprising revelation
6. **Takeaways** (1.5 min): What listeners should remember
7. **Closing** (30 sec): Sign off with a thought-provoking question

## Conversation Dynamics
- Alex asks questions a curious listener would ask
- Sam explains with analogies and real-world examples
- Include moments of genuine surprise: "Wait, really?"
- Light disagreements make it interesting
- Humor should feel natural, not forced
- Reference specific data from the report
- Keep turns short (2-4 sentences) for natural pacing

## Audio Optimization
- Avoid overly long sentences (hard for TTS)
- Use conversational contractions (don't, it's, we're)
- Include natural interjections (Well, So, Right, Exactly)
- Spell out numbers and abbreviations
- Avoid parenthetical asides
