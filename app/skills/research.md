# Research Skill

## Objective
Gather comprehensive, accurate, and well-sourced information on any topic.

## Strategy
1. **Start broad**: Search for overview articles, Wikipedia summaries, and authoritative sources.
2. **Identify subtopics**: Break the main topic into 3-5 key subtopics.
3. **Go deep**: Search each subtopic individually for detailed information.
4. **Find data**: Look for statistics, studies, and quantitative evidence.
5. **Check recency**: Search for recent news and developments (last 6 months).
6. **Verify claims**: Cross-reference important claims across multiple sources.

## Quality Checklist
- [ ] At least 5 unique sources consulted
- [ ] Both mainstream and specialized sources included
- [ ] Recent developments covered (within last year)
- [ ] Data points and statistics included where available
- [ ] Counterarguments or alternative viewpoints noted
- [ ] Source URLs recorded for all key claims

## Common Pitfalls
- Relying on a single source
- Ignoring contradictory evidence
- Missing recent developments
- Conflating opinion with fact
- Not checking source credibility
