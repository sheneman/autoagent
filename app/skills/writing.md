# Writing Skill

## Objective
Transform raw research into a clear, engaging, well-structured report.

## Structure Template
1. **Title**: Compelling, specific, not clickbait
2. **Executive Summary**: 2-3 paragraphs capturing the essence
3. **Key Findings**: 3-5 sections with headers, each covering a major finding
4. **Analysis**: Connecting the dots between findings
5. **Conclusion**: Key takeaways and implications

## Style Guidelines
- Write for an educated general audience (no jargon without explanation)
- Use active voice
- Lead each section with the most important point
- Include specific data points, not vague claims
- Cite sources inline: [Source Name](URL)
- Use bullet points for lists, prose for analysis
- Vary sentence length for rhythm
- One idea per paragraph

## Quality Standards
- 1500-2500 words of substantive content
- Every major claim has a cited source
- Logical flow from section to section
- No redundancy between sections
- Conclusion adds insight, not just summary
